# 🚀 Sovereign Agent: Public Launch & Usage Guide

Welcome to the Sovereign Agent. This guide will help you get fully set up, configure your AI brain, and start applying to jobs powerfully.

---

## 📥 Step 1: Download & Install

> If you are looking for specific OS installation instructions (Windows, Mac, Linux), please read our **[Multi-Platform Setup Guide](SETUP_GUIDE.md)**.

For Windows users using the Portable Executable:
1.  Go to the **[Latest Releases](https://github.com/wisdomgreat/JobAutomation/releases/latest)** page.
2.  Download the file named `SovereignAgent_Portable.zip` and extract it to a folder.
3.  **Double-click** `SovereignAgent_Portable.exe` to launch. 

---

## ⚙️ Step 2: Full Onboarding (First Launch)

When you first open Sovereign Agent, you need to configure your profile so the AI knows who you are and how to represent you. 

### 1. Connecting the AI Brain (LLM & API Keys)
Sovereign Agent uses an external AI (LLM) to read job descriptions and write customized cover letters for you.
1. Navigate to the **System Core** tab (the gear icon on the left sidebar).
2. Under the **AI Provider Engine**, select your preferred provider from the dropdown (e.g., `OpenAI`, `Anthropic`, or `OpenRouter` for cost-effective models).
3. Choose your specific model from the secondary dropdown (e.g., `gpt-4o-mini`).
4. Paste your API Key into the **Provider API Key** box and click **SAVE KEY**.
> *Tip: You can verify your connection is working by clicking the **"Test AI Synapse"** button!*

### 2. Building Your Identity Profile
The AI needs to know your career history to apply effectively.
1. Still in the **System Core** tab, click **EDIT GLOBAL IDENTITY**.
2. This opens the **Identity Commander**. Fill out your personal details, job preferences (Target Roles), demographics, and experience.
3. Once finished, click the large **[ ✓ ] SAVE IDENTITY** button to finalize your profile.

### 3. Uploading Your Master Resume
The AI needs your base resume to tailor it for specific jobs.
1. Navigate to the **Asset Hub** tab.
2. Under the **Master Assets Component**, click the button to **Upload Resume**.
3. Select your current PDF or Word Document resume.
> *Note: The system will store this securely on your computer and use it as the foundation for all future applications and tailoring.*

---

## 🎯 Step 3: Running Your First Target Scan

With your AI connected and profile uploaded, you are ready to find jobs!

1. Navigate to the **Target Scan** tab.
2. In the **Search Matrix**, enter your desired **Job Title** (e.g., `Python Developer`) and **Location** (e.g., `Remote` or `Chicago, IL`).
3. Select your desired platforms by checking the boxes for **LinkedIn**, **Indeed**, etc.
4. Click the large **🚀 RUN FULL AUTO-PIPELINE** button.
5. Watch the **Dashboard** tab! The agent will launch a stealth browser in the background, extract jobs, calculate your match scores, and sync them to your local database.

---

## ✉️ Step 4: Applying and Managing Candidates

Once a scan finishes, jobs are automatically routed to your internal tracker.

### How to Apply:
1. Navigate to the **Candidate CRM** tab.
2. Review the jobs in your feed. The AI will automatically highlight your **Match Score** alongside the job requirements.
3. **To Apply Quickly**: Click the **⚡ Quick Apply** button on any job card. The AI will instantly generate a customized cover letter and tailored resume specifically for that job and place it in your `output/` folder.
4. **Surgical Precision**: If you found a specific job url online and want to target it directly, go to the **Target Scan** tab, paste the URL into the **Surgical Precision Strike** box, and click **GENERATE NEW KIT**.
5. After your customized assets are generated, click **Open Output Folder** to view them and submit them to the employer's portal.
6. Finally, click the **[ ✓ ]** button in the CRM feed to mark the job as 'Applied' and move it out of your queue.

## 📧 Bonus: The Email Inbox Monitor (App Passwords)

To use the advanced **Email Scanner** (which reads job alert emails and syncs them as targets), you must connect your email via the security tab. For safety, this requires an **App Password**.

### 🔑 How to get an App Password:

#### **1. Gmail / Google**
1. Go to your [Google Account Security](https://myaccount.google.com/security) settings.
2. Enable 2-Step Verification.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the 2-step verification page and click **App passwords**.
4. Name it "Sovereign Agent" and click Create.
5. Paste the 16-character code into the Sovereign Agent's **Security Strategy / Gmail** tab.

#### **2. Yahoo Mail**
1. Log in to your [Yahoo Security](https://login.yahoo.com/account/security) page.
2. Click **Generate app password**.
3. Select **Other App**, name it "Sovereign Agent", and generate.

#### **3. Outlook / Microsoft**
1. Go to [Security basics](https://account.microsoft.com/security).
2. Select **Advanced security options**.
3. Under App passwords, select **Create a new app password**.

## 🛡️ Best Practices & Architecture
* **100% Local**: All your passwords, API keys, and resumes stay on **your** computer. We do not use telemetry.
* **Human Mimicry**: The agent scrolls, pauses, and acts like a real person to protect your LinkedIn/Indeed accounts from bans.
